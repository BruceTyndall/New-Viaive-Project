# VIAIVE Product Context

register: brand

## Product Purpose

VIAIVE is a luxury travel decision-and-intake platform. The homepage is the flagship proof object: it must communicate editorial authority, route travelers into the right advisory desk, support Stay22/hotel monetization where appropriate, and convert high-intent visitors into private briefs.

## Source Of Truth

The binding design and implementation package lives in `docs/figma/source/`. When there is tension between generic frontend convention and the package, the package wins. Use the package docs, JSON exports, source components, and screenshots before making visual decisions.

## Audience

VIAIVE is for premium travelers who do not want a marketplace, blog archive, or booking-engine experience. They need a quiet advisory desk, hotel intelligence, destination judgment, and a clear next step.

## Brand Voice

Restrained, cinematic, editorial, private, decisive. VIAIVE should feel like engraved stationery for serious travelers, not a SaaS landing page or an affiliate marketplace.

## Anti-References

Avoid generic travel-blog cards, purple gradients, rounded SaaS panels, nested card soup, stock icon grids, noisy affiliate widgets, weak contrast, pure black/white neutrals, and excessive repeated scaffolding that reads as AI-generated.

## Build Principle

Homepage fidelity comes before route expansion. `/` must preserve the Figma component spine and map cleanly to Payload fields before broader pages are scaled.
